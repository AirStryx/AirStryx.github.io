Introduction

Hyper Dash is a free VR multiplayer team based shooter. Make smart use of Dashing, Sprinting, Walking and Rail Grinding to get around the arenas and win in a variety of gamemodes like; Payload, Domination, Control Point, (Team) Deathmatch, Capture The Flag, Elimination & Ball.

I worked on the prototype for Hyperdash during my end of school internship.

Technical Details

Engine: Unity

Multiplayer Solution: Photon PUN (prototype) Photon Bolt (production)

Platform: Meta, Steam, Pico (PC, Android)

My Role

  • Started from an idea and a very grounded simple demo that had a few days of work, us interns had to prove it could be a fun and viable game worth to build out.
  • Prototype multiple movement mechanics to see what would be fun and not nauseating for the general consumer base.
  • Testing multiple ways to handle weapons, guns attached to the holsters, guns not attached at all to finally end up with guns stuck to the hands.
  • Learn about optimized networking, where to "cut corners" to give players the smoothest experience.
