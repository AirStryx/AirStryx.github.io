Introduction

Crystal Mech is a Top-Down Sci-Fi-Magic Bullet Hell. It's designed as a co-operative game for 1-4 players, created by a team of 8 people [4 programmers, 4 artists] for the annual DAE Crea-Week Game Jam. This Game Jam started on Wednesday-morning, the 2nd of May 2018, and lasted until Friday the 4th of May at noon, so all in all that's 2 and a half days of work! The theme of the game jam was "Magic & 3 Rules".

Technical Details

Engine: Unreal Engine 4 (Blueprinting)

Multiplayer Solution: Local

Platform: PC (Check it out on Itch.io)

My Role

  • Good practice on iterative work within a pretty big team without any central managers.
  • Help with fast decision making on both design, gameplay and delegating work.
  • Implement shooting mechanics and the bullet reflection mechanics.
